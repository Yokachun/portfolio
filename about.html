About Yoka Chun.

I'm a UI/UX designer based in Hong Kong. Most recently, I was working at PRIZM Group, a digital agency where I was designing web platform and mobile application. Prior to that, I also worked at WECREATE, a design agencies where I helped clients create better digital experiences.

My Design Philosophy

My design philosophy is to take peoples needs in the first place. I ma a firm believer of thorough research, wireframing, prototyping, testing, and iterating to get the best user experiences possible.

Design thinking is a non-linear, iterative process that teams use to understand users, challenge assumptions, redefine problems and create innovative solutions to prototype and test. Involving five phases—Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype and Test—it is most useful to tackle problems that are ill-defined or unknown.
