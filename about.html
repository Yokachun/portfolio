About Yoka Chun

I’m always hungry to create new user experiences. My long-term goal is to make technology easy to use and accessible to everyone. The motivation that drives me daily is to work with people and brands that inspire me.

What's My Misson?

My expertise covers the entire digital product design process for both websites and mobile apps, including brand and visual communication, user experience design and user interface design. I like building new products from scratch or optimizing existing ones so they perform better from both a user and business perspective.

Product design vision and conceptual design are the stages I enjoy working on the most, and I recently had a chance to do exactly that for clients like Toyota, Nescafe, Wyeth, AlipayHK, and others.

What I Do

Working in a number of digital projects from well-established companies to improve and add move value to their products through design.

UX Design

UI Design

Branding & Identity
