Ngong Ping 360 -

An awesome walking tour app

ROLE App UX & UI Design
CLIENT Ngong Ping 360

Toyota’s legend Supra was launched between May to August in 2019. Pre-orders allow customer to reserve an item from store before it's officially available. The portal helps the brand get a guaranteed number of sales and revenue within an estimated time period.

We have been approached by Toyota to build up its web presence with an unique User Interface that would solidify the brand identity. The main objective was to improve the user experience design while respecting the brand’s existing ethos and aesthetic as well as the back-end structures.
